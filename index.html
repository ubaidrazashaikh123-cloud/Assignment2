Places I have visited.

  • Islamabad

1. Murree

I recently visited Murree, and it was truly an unforgettable experience. The cool breeze, lush green hills, and breathtaking views brought so much peace to the heart. From the lively Mall Road to the Patriata chair lift, every spot had its own unique charm. The pleasant weather and natural beauty of Murree made my trip even more special. This journey will always remain one of the best memories of my life.

2. Hunza

Visiting Hunza felt like stepping into a paradise on earth. Surrounded by majestic snow-capped mountains, crystal-clear rivers, and peaceful valleys, Hunza offered a sense of calm that words can't fully describe. The warm hospitality of the locals, along with the breathtaking sights of Karimabad and Attabad Lake, made this journey truly magical. Hunza is a dream destination that stays with you forever.

3. China Border

My journey to the Pakistan-China border at Khunjerab Pass was extraordinary. At one of the world's highest paved international borders, the breathtaking mountains and freezing winds created an unforgettable adventure. Standing there, surrounded by snowy peaks and vast landscapes, I felt both small and amazed by nature's greatness. The experience was once in a lifetime.

4. Islamabad

My trip to Islamabad was refreshing and full of beauty. The city, surrounded by the Margalla Hills, blends modern living with nature perfectly. From the serenity of Faisal Mosque to the scenic views at Daman-e-Koh, every corner felt peaceful and well-organized. Islamabad's greenery, wide roads, and calm environment make it one of the most beautiful capitals in the world

5. lahore

Lahore is a city of culture, history, and unmatched energy. My visit was filled with exploring the grand Badshahi Mosque, the historic Lahore Fort, and the colorful streets of the old city. The food of Lahore, from spicy street snacks to mouth-watering traditional dishes, added a whole new flavor to the trip. Lahore is truly the heart of Pakistan, where history and life go hand in hand.

6. Karachi

Visiting Karachi was like experiencing the hustle and bustle of life at its peak. The city never sleeps - from the calm evenings at Clifton Beach to the busy markets of Saddar and Tariq Road, everything had its own charm. The sea breeze, diverse culture, and delicious food made Karachi feel alive and full of opportunities. It's a city where tradition meets modernity.

1. Makkah

Visiting Makkah was the most spiritual and heart-touching journey of my life. Standing before the Holy Kaaba, surrounded by Muslims from all around the world, I felt a deep sense of peace, unity, and connection with Allah. Performing Umrah in the sacred city was a truly blessed experience that I will cherish forever.

2. Madinah

My visit to Madinah was filled with tranquility and love. The peaceful atmosphere around Masjid an-Nabawi, the resting place of our beloved Prophet ï·º, touched my heart deeply. Walking through the illuminated streets of Madinah gave me comfort and serenity that cannot be found anywhere else. It is truly the city of peace

3. Jeddah

Jeddah, known as the gateway to Makkah and Madinah, was a beautiful mix of tradition and modern life. I enjoyed the Corniche along the Red Sea, the vibrant markets, and the city's lively culture. Jeddah is full of history and also offers a modern lifestyle, making it a unique destination to experience.

4. Riyadh

Riyadh, the capital of Saudi Arabia, amazed me with its modern skyscrapers, rich culture, and historical landmarks. From the Kingdom Centre Tower to the National Museum, the city reflected both tradition and progress. Riyadh's energy, development, and hospitality made my visit memorable and inspiring.
